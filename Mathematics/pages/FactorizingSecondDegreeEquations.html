Quadratic trinomials

Quadratic trinomials are a kind of equations that contains a quadratic term, a linear term, and a lone number.

    This is an example of a quadratic trinomial and its factorization.

    1. $x^2 + bx + c = (x+h)(x+k)$

    the right side of the equation is the factorization where $h*k=c$ and $h+k=b$;

    Depending on the sign of the terms of the equation h and k can be positive or negative.



    2. $ax +bx+c = (mx+h)(nx+k)$
